Sam Bruner

Sam Bruner is an undergraduate student at Illinois State University studying Creative Technology. She is currently on track to graduate in December 2020. As a creative she enjoys writing, coding, sound design, and design in general. She also Co-Founded the literary magazine Sonder Midwest in 2017. Sonder Midwest is a project that allows her to provide a safe space for under represented creatives to present their work while also helping her give back to a community that has given so much to her.

She founder her passion for the literary world in 2016 when she was apart of a writing internship with Midwest Writing Center based out of Rock Island, Illinois. During this summer long internship she worked hard to refine her craft as well as help put together the literary magazine The Atlas 11 with her colleagues. She went on to hold this internship the following two summers.

If she's not working on Sonder's next issue you can find her writing, reading, playing guitar, enjoying time outside or petting dogs.
